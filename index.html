L5Modular

Keep Your Laravel App Organized

Get Started → Introduction

Organized Codebase

The main goal of L5Modular is to build better organized projects by separate the project into modules.

Reusable Modules

A nice side effect is the reusability of the modules. There are often enough projects where you don't have to reinvent the wheel.

Free & Open Source

L5Modular is an MIT licensed open source project. It's fully tested and has a highly ranked code quality.
